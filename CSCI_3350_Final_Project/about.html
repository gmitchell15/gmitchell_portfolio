Mitchell Florist is an established flower shop based in Clarksville, Tennessee, USA. It has been in the flower and gift shop business for the past 10 years. We mainly sell flowers and gifts, but we also deliver flowers. We also sell flower arrangements for special occasions and gift basks upon request. Mitchell Florist is a small team and regularly hires contractors.

Mitchell Florist was started because of our owner's love of nature photography. Mitchell Florist strives to be your first choice when you need flowers. We have all kinds of flowers for any occasion from birthdays to anniversaries. If you can't make it to the store, we also deliver.
