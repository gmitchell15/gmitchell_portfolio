Exhibitions

Pellissippi State Community College regularly has exhibitions in the Bagwell show room. In addition to student work, professional work is also shown. You can still have work shown even after you have graduated. Each major, like Photography, have at least one exhibitions a semester. There is also the Annual Juried Exhibition where students in visual art classes can submit work to be displayed. Awards are given out such as first place having their work purchased by the college for $500.
